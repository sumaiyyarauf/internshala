Admission Process & Eligibility

Follow these simple steps to secure your MBBS admission abroad.

To apply for MBBS abroad, you generally need: - A minimum of 50% marks in 12th grade (PCB). - NEET qualification (for Indian students). - Age requirement: 17+ years.

Documents needed: - 10th & 12th mark sheets - Passport copy - NEET scorecard (if required) - Passport-sized photos - Medical fitness certificate

Once your documents are verified, you will receive an **Admission Letter** from the university. The next step is to pay the initial tuition fee to confirm your seat.

Apply for a **student visa** with: - University admission letter - Valid passport - Visa application form - Medical test results - Financial proof (Bank statement)

- Book your flight tickets once you receive the **student visa**. - Pack essentials (documents, medical insurance, etc.). - Arrive at your university and complete the registration process. - Start your MBBS journey! 🎉
